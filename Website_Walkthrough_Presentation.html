A Digital Hub for the Community

  • Centralized Information – A single source of truth for all center activities.
  • Accessibility – Designed for everyone, from elders to youth.

Slide 3: Navigation Experience

Seamless Navigation

  • Intuitive Navbar – 'Pill' design for clean separation of tools.
  • Language Accessibility – Dedicated English/Arabic switcher.
  • Quick Links – Social media and contact info always visible.
  • Smart Dropdowns – Organized menus for Services, Forms, and About Us.
Speaker Note:
“Let's start at the top. The navigation bar features a unique 'pill' design. This separates functional tools—like the Language Switcher and Social Media icons—from the main menu, keeping things clutter-free. The dropdown menus are categorized intelligently, so whether you're looking for 'Services' or 'Forms', you find exactly what you need in seconds without deep searching.”
